Problem Statement

One-sentence: Students at USC who have to use Blackboard, GitHub, and Discord for schoolwork need one simple place to see all their assignments, deadlines, and group tasks so they don’t lose track or miss work.

Full problem statement (click to expand)

Who: College students at the University of South Carolina who take multiple classes and work on group projects through Blackboard, GitHub, and Discord.

What: It’s hard to keep track of everything when all the due dates, messages, and files are spread across different apps. Students end up missing deadlines or forgetting tasks.

Where: Most of this happens when students are studying, in class, or working on projects from their laptops or phones.

Why: When everything is in different places, it’s stressful and confusing. Having to switch between apps takes time and makes it easy to forget what’s due or who’s doing what.

Constraints: The solution should work with the tools students already use instead of replacing them. It should also be easy to check quickly from a phone or laptop.

How we’ll know it works: Students miss fewer deadlines, it’s faster to find what needs to be done, and teams feel more organized and know what everyone’s working on.
