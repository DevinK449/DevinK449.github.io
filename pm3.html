Problem Statement

Students at the University of South Carolina who use multiple platforms such as Blackboard, GitHub, and Discord for schoolwork need one central location to view all their assignments, deadlines, and group tasks so they don’t lose track or miss important work.

Category 1 — Root Causes

  • Too many platforms for coursework communication
  • Lack of integration between Blackboard, GitHub, and Discord
  • Students forget to check all apps daily
  • No central reminder or alert system
  • Hard to keep track of group progress

Category 2 — Constraints

  • Limited time for students to manage multiple tasks
  • Different systems require separate logins
  • Mobile versions of apps are not equally reliable
  • Students rely on inconsistent group communication

Category 3 — Opportunities

  • Create a unified dashboard to view all due dates and tasks
  • Integrate GitHub deadlines with Blackboard calendar
  • Add push notifications for upcoming due dates
  • Improve collaboration with shared progress tracking
  • Use APIs to sync data automatically between apps

Category 4 — Ideas & Solutions

  • Develop a web app that connects Blackboard, GitHub, and Discord
  • Implement color-coded assignment lists by class
  • Add notifications through Discord bots or email alerts
  • Include a group chat and task status tracker
  • Allow professors to post announcements through one integrated hub

How to use

These clusters summarize what we observed in PM2: the Root Causes of disorganization, Constraints that make it difficult to manage multiple platforms, Opportunities for streamlining information, and final Ideas & Solutions for improvement.
